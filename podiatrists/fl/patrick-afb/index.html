About podiatry

Podiatrists (DPM) diagnose and treat foot and ankle problems. Common reasons to visit include heel pain, bunions, ingrown toenails, sports injuries, and diabetic foot care.

Common conditions

  • Plantar Fasciitis
  • Bunions
  • Ingrown Toenails
  • Heel Pain
  • Diabetic Foot Care

Common treatments

  • Custom Orthotics
  • Foot Surgery
  • Nail Procedures
  • Shockwave Therapy
